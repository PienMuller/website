If there’s something I could talk about for days it would be magical girls, and this is an entire website dedicated to this popular genre. I will talk about the history of magical girls, popular magical girls in today’s media and magical girls in general

From fighting crimes and sweet love stories to murderous fiends and becoming literal god, the magical girl genre is much more than just young girls fighting in cute uniforms. The genre itself is more of a recipe with room for variation and a creator’s freedom, allowing these stories to be as dark as they are cute, giving us shows like Madoka Magica and Magical Girls Raising Project.

Allow me to introduce you to this fascinating genre and teach you about the cute yet surprisingly dark world of magical girls.
